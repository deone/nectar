How To Order

Opening An Account

We would need the following information in order to set up your business account:

  • Certificate of Incorporation
  • Proof of Registered Business Operations
  • Tax Clearance
  • Company Name and Telephone Number
  • Government Issued ID ( International Passport or Driving Licence Preferred)

Shipping Policy

Thank you for your business and your continued support.

Orders placed before 11:00am will in most cases shipped out same day depending on the state. - All delivery is a client's cost and liability. Clients are encouraged to pick up from the Nectar Beauty Hub Office location on appointments only.

Minimum Freight Order

Please contact us for more information about minimum orders for delivery. Any order less than that should be picked up. To waive freight charges, a minimum freight amount must be placed. Minimum weight and cost conditions are applicable.

We accept VISA and MasterCard.
